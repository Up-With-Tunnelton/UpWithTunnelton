Our Purpose:


We are Up With Tunnelton. A non-profit community organization operating under the laws of the state of West Virginia.
The purpose of our organization includes, but is not limited to: ensuring that ordinary people have a way to come together around issues of common concern and take action towards
resolving those issues, supporting and conducting events and activities to promote pride and deter juvenile delinquency, providing opportunities for fellowship
and create worthwhile relationships within our community, and preventing community deterioration.
